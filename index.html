About University

The Pak-Austria Fachhochschule Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a higher education institution in Pakistan that focuses on applied sciences and technology. It was established through collaboration between Pakistan and Austria, aiming to promote education and research in various scientific and technological fields.
